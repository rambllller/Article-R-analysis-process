- README.md
- .gitignore
- metadata/groups.tsv.example
- scripts/01_run_humann_metacyc.sh
- scripts/02_merge_metacyc.py
- scripts/03_lefse_metacyc.R
- scripts/04_maaslin2_metacyc.R

- scripts/06_merge_buglist.py

## Do not upload
- Raw FASTQ files
- Large HUMAnN3 result folders
- Personal absolute paths
- Temporary logs from the server
- Full unpublished datasets unless journal policy requires them

## Confirm before manuscript submission
- The manuscript says Maaslin2 or MaAsLin3 consistently
- Group labels in README match the real analysis
- Reference level is correct
- Database names/versions are recorded in Methods or Supplementary Materials
